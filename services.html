Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get flowers delivered today?

Usually, yes! Call or text us at before 2:30 PM and we can almost always arrange same-day delivery.

Where do you deliver, and what does it cost?

We deliver up to 30 miles — homes, businesses, schools, hospitals, churches, and funeral homes. Delivery is $5 in Canton, $10 just out of town, and $15 to Wills Point, Grand Saline, Van, Mabank, Martins Mill & Ben Wheeler.

What if a flower in the photo isn't available?

Flowers are seasonal, so exact blooms can vary with what's freshest that day. If we need to substitute, we always match the style, color feel, and value of what you ordered — and we'll call you first if it's a big change.

Can I send you a picture of what I want?

Please do! Text your inspiration photos to or email them to — Pinterest finds, a photo of the dress, an arrangement you loved. We'll design from it and match colors as closely as the season allows.

Do you do silk arrangements?

Yes — silk is one of our specialties. Silk arrangements are beautiful keepsakes for cemeteries, home décor, and anyone who wants their flowers to last.

How far ahead should I book wedding flowers?

Please book at least two months in advance. A 50% retainer holds your date, and the balance is due two weeks before the wedding. Shorter timeline? Call anyway — we'll tell you honestly what we can do.

How do I pay?

Cash, check, debit and all major credit cards, and Zelle. Pay over the phone when you order, or in person at the shop.

Can you keep flowers at a loved one's grave year-round?

Yes — ask about our cemetery subscription. You pick the dates (birthdays, holidays, anniversaries), and on each one we place a fresh silk arrangement ($60) at your loved one's resting place — then send you a photo, so you always know it's been done.
